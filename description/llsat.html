About the Author

Kate Forsyth is the internationally bestselling author of 40 books for both adults and children. Her books for adults include "Beauty in Thorns", "The Beast's Garden", "The Wild Girl", "Bitter Greens", and the bestselling fantasy series "Witches of Eileanan". Her books for children include "The Impossible Quest", "The Gypsy Crown", "The Puzzle Ring", and "The Starkin Crown". Kate has a doctorate in fairytale studies, a Masters of Creative Writing, a Bachelor of Arts in Literature, and is an accredited master storyteller.
